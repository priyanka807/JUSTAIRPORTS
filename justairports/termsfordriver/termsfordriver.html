32 Fares

32.1 You agree the pre-defined tariff and charges laid down by Justairports is sufficient for you to make enough remuneration to pay for all expenses and save enough for your livelihood i.e equal or more than national minimum wage. 32.2 You agree that Justairports will collect the fare on your behalf (through its website) for each Credit / Debit card paid Journeys.

32.3 The actual Fare for each Journey provided by You will be determined by the Justairports, having regard to the actual journey time, distance travelled and delays etc.

32.4 A Passenger will be offered to use a Journey option that allows the Passenger to agree to a fix parking charges on credit / debit card paid bookings. In those cases You will not ask / request / demand passengers for extra amount on pretext of parking charges. In case you had to pay more for parking charges, Justairports will reimburse you for those parking charges on pickup from Airports only, once you deliver original parking receipt to Justairports registered office. Justairports will not pay for any parking charges where the passenger(s) were not picked up from an airport. For further clarification Justairports only charges Passenger(s) Pre paid parking charges for Journeys in Which Pickup point is from an airport.

32.5 You may complete a Journey by any reasonable route, and Justairports does not set any route restrictions. However, You commit to Justairports and each Passenger that You will provide the Journey which may be shortest or fastest in distance. Where any Journey You completed did not comply wilfully with this Term, Justairports reserves the right to have Justairports set-off any refunds or compensation to the affected Passenger(s) against Your Justairports Payments to you. In other words Justairports has the right to deduct this compensation / refund amount from your account.

32.6 A Passenger may cancel a request for a Journey that a Driver has accepted. The Driver is entitled to a Justairports Payment for a cancelled Journey(“Cancellation Fee”) in the event that a Passenger cancels an allocated booking for a Journey within 1 hours of the pickup time and only if Justairports was able to recover the cancelation fees. The amount of cancelation fees will be decided by Justairports and the decision of this amount will binding for You to accept.

33 Payment

33.1 Payment mode accepted for every booking is Cash or Credit / Debit card.

33.2 You will be responsible to collect cash payment from passenger(s). You will also issue the receipt the Passenger(s) for this amount.

33.3 Payments accepted via online Credit / Debit card mode will be settled once a week on a pre determined day of the week.

33.4 Justairports shall invoice the Passenger on Your behalf. Justairports will then create a receipt on Your behalf setting out the Fare, time and other relevant information relating to that particular Journey.

33.5 The receipt will be forwarded to the Passenger for online credit / debit card payments.

33.6 In the event that You or passenger dispute the amount Justairports may withhold the disputed amounts until the dispute has been resolved amicably.

33.7 Without prejudice to clause 32.9, You acknowledge that Justairports shall not be obliged to pay You the cash Payments due from a Fare in the event of non-payment for whatever reason or if the Passenger(s) failed to pay because whatever reason, not attributable to Justairports. If this occurs, Justairports will help You in requesting the Fare due from the Passenger in respect of the Journey. However, if Justairports is unable to collect the Fare for whatever reason, Justairports may decide not to pay You or may decide to pay You Payment in whole or in part if it considers it is reasonable to do so. In any event, Justairports will not pay more than £25 per any one Journey. Justairports decision will be final.

33.8 Your Justairports Payments reports will be made available to You on a weekly basis. The reports will show the Fares collected on your behalf in relation to Your Journeys, as well as the amounts of the Commission withheld by Justairports.

33.9 Without prejudice to clause 32.7, you are responsible for the risk of non-payment of Fares for Extensive Journeys (as defined in this clause). Justairports will help You in requesting the Fare due from the Passenger for an Extensive Journey in the event of non-payment, and Justairports will reimburse a Payment to You if the Fare is ultimately collected from the Passenger. However, if Justairports determines that the trip is fraudulent or is unable to collect the Fare for an Extensive Journey from a Passenger after making one attempt to do so, no Payment will be made to you in respect of that Journey. In this clause, an “Extensive Journey” means a Passenger journey in excess of 90 miles in distance and/or two hours in duration.
